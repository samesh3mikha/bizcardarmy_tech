All cards are processed and reviewed by highly qualified workers. We guarantee 100% accuracy on our transcription service. However, if the business card you upload is partly illegible, the illegible parts will be ignored.

How are business cards organized in the iphone app?

The cards are basically categorized as pending, completed and unpaid. When you upload a card, its status is 'pending'. If you don't have credits for payment, the cards you upload are marked as 'unpaid' and are not processed until payment is done. Once the cards have been transcribed, it is 'completed'. Also, you can sort you cards using different options like name, company, created date etc.

What are Bizcardarmy credits?

You need to buy credits to transcribe your cards. Each card you upload will cost you one credit. Each credit costs you $0.25.

Can I test the application before I buy credits?

Yes. We give you 3 free credits at the beginning when you sign up.

How can I buy credits?

You can also buy credits via our web application at www.bizcardarmy.com with your current account.

How do I export the digitized cards to my iphone address book?

The digitized cards are automatically converted to an address contact. So you don't have to worry about this.

Can I delete the pending or unpaid cards?

No, you are not allowed to delete pending or unpaid cards. You can only delete the completed cards.

What happens to the addressbook contact if I delete a completed card from the application?

The addressbook contact inserted by Bizcardarmy will be intact even if you delete the card from the application.

What happens to the unpaid cards once I purchase credits?

The unpaid cards will be automatically sent for processing and the purchase credits will be used.
